Hello, My name is Harris Goldstein.

One of my biggest passions is storytelling, so I have compiled some of the stories I have created over the years for anyone to view and enjoy.

Inner Demons

Inner Demons is a story of a woman attempting to deal with trauma and adjust to life after it has been fundamentally changed in the span of a single night.

Chimera

Chimera is one of my darker stories following Vlad Petros, a former soldier who agrees to become a government lab rat in exchange for his sister's safety: however, when the experiment is over, will Vlad still retain his humanity or will he become something more monstrous?

The Conjuror

The Conjuror was the first complex story that I have written, and its premise was what would happen if someone gained the ability that everything they drew became real. I plan to revisit this story and turn it into a novel as some of my ideas cannot translate to a static visual format. Also, reading it again, I definitely need to make some scenes/dialogue less corny.

Psychopomp

Psychopomp is a story of the day-to-day life of a grim reaper who acts as a therapist for the recently deceased, helping them move on to their respected afterlife, all while still processing her own death.

Shadow

Shadow is the story of Bryan Cohen, a societal reject who only has one friend, his shadow; however, one day his shadow talks back.
